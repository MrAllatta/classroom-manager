## Calendar Notes

> **Regents Prep Overlap (structural calendar tension):** The 2026-27 NYC DOE calendar between September 9, 2026 and June 30, 2027 contains **179 non-blackout weekdays**. After reserving 10 buffer days, **169 net instructional slots** are available — one fewer than the 170 instructional days specified. As a result, the 170th instructional day falls on **2027-06-30**, and Units 9 and 10 run concurrently with or after the June 5–13 Regents prep window. Teachers should treat Unit 9 and Unit 10 delivery during June 5–30 as **light review and application** framing, integrating Regents prep rather than displacing it. The Unit 9 and Unit 10 end-of-unit assessments (June 10 and June 30 respectively) may be administered as cumulative/Regents-style assessments.

> **Standards Alignment:** NY Next Gen Mathematics Learning Standards alignment for each unit is **deferred pending teacher specification**. This calendar governs pacing only; standards mapping will be completed in a subsequent planning artifact.

> **Section Pacing:** Sections ALG-01 (Period 1), ALG-02 (Period 2), and ALG-03 (Period 4) all meet every instructional day and follow **identical pacing**. No differentiation by section is required at the calendar level.
